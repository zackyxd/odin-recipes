Tuna Rice

Description

Ever need a quick meal? Tuna, rice, and soy sauce is all you need!

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Cans of tuna
  • Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Cook your rice however you like
  2. In a bowl, put the rice and adds however many cans of tuna you'd like
  3. Put in some soy sauce and mix
  4. Enjoy!
