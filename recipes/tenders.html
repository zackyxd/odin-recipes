Chicken Tenders

Description

Quickly make crispy chicken tenders that are healthier than store-bought options!

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp hot paprika
  • 2 tsp onion and garlic powder
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 200g all-purpose flour
  • 135g cornstarch
  • 2.65 lbs chicken breast

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400F
  2. Cut the chicken into thin strips
  3. Put the chicken into a bowl and season with half of the seasonings above and mix
  4. In a different bowl mix the flour, cornstarch, and the other half of the seasonings
  5. Put 1 cup of the flour mixture and 1 cup of water into a different bowl and mix
  6. Put 3-5 tbsp of the dry/wet mixture into the dry mix and mix it until crumbly
  7. Coat the chicken in the dry mix, in the wet mix, then into the dry mix again. Place on a baking sheet
  8. Make the sauce to spread with: low heat on stovetop with 85 ml of buffalo sauce and 40g of honey
  9. When chicken is done, evenly spread the sauce on top/bottom of the chicken
  10. Serve and enjoy!
