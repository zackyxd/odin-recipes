Pretzels

Description

This recipe will have you making a homemade pretzel from scratch. These ingredients are very common in households. You can make this about 1 1/2 hours!

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup baking soda
  • 2 tbsp granulated sugar
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • Vegetable oil for greasing the bowl
  • 1 tbsp coarse pretzel salt (or kosher)
  • 8 tbsp butter

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 250F with a baking sheet spread evenly with 2/3 cup of baking soda
  2. Melt 2 tbsp of butter
  3. Put 1 cup warm water into a semi-large bowl
  4. Whisk in 2 tbsp of sugar into the bowl until dissolved
  5. Sprinkle in the entire yeast packet and let it sit for 5 minutes
  6. Add all the flour slowly and mix it until a dough forms, and make sure it's mostly smooth
  7. Pick up the dough and spray the bowl with some oil, then let it sit somewhere warm for 45 minutes to an hour
  8. When the baking soda is done, set it somewhere and preheat oven to 450F
  9. Dump the baking soda into a new bowl of 3 cups of hot water and mix until mostly dissolved
  10. When dough is done, pour it out and slice into even pieces
  11. Cut the strips into 1 1/2 inches wide and role into 'snakes'. Make a pretzel shape
  12. Dip the pretzels into the water and let it drip off, place on a baking sheet
  13. When all pretzels are done, sprinkle evenly with coarse salt
  14. Put pretzels into oven for 8 minutes. Rotate the sheet halfway through.
  15. Melt the remaining butter and brush the pretzels when they are finished
  16. Serve and enjoy!
